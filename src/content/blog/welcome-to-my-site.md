I'm excited to finally launch this personal site. After months of building, researching, and iterating, it feels great to have a dedicated space to share my work and ideas.

## What to Expect

This blog will serve as a space for several things:

- **Research updates** — Progress on my current papers in process mining, Petri nets, and pool testing algorithms.
- **Technical deep dives** — Explorations of topics in algorithm design, mathematical modeling, and data science.
- **AI & Policy reflections** — Thoughts on how artificial intelligence intersects with public policy, drawing from my dual background in physics and political science.
- **Learning in public** — Sharing what I learn as I go, from new programming techniques to research methodologies.

## Why a Blog?

As someone who studies both physics and political science, I've come to appreciate the value of communicating complex ideas clearly. A blog is the perfect medium for that — it forces you to think through your ideas thoroughly and present them in a way that others can engage with.

I believe that the most interesting work happens at the boundaries between disciplines, and I hope this space reflects that philosophy.

Stay tuned for more posts. If you'd like to discuss any of the topics I cover, don't hesitate to [reach out](/contact).
